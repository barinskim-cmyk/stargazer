STARGAZER
Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026
Short version: Stargazer does not collect personal data, does not track you, and does not sell your information to anyone. The constellations you create can optionally be published to a shared sky — if you do, only the constellation data and a random position are stored.

1. Who We Are

Stargazer is an indie game developed independently. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at: barinski.m@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary for the app to function:

  • Constellation data — the star patterns you draw during the game. Stored locally on your device.
  • Published constellation data — if you choose to publish a constellation, its drawing data, name, and a randomly assigned position in the shared sky are sent to our database (Supabase). No personal information is attached unless you sign in.
  • Anonymous user ID — if you sign in with Apple or Google, we store an anonymous identifier to associate your constellations with your account. We never receive your name or email unless you explicitly provide them.
  • Game progress — your rank, score, and settings are stored locally using your device's storage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number (unless you sign in with Apple/Google — in which case we only receive an anonymous ID).
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics platforms.
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Stargazer uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase — an open-source backend platform used to store published constellations. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Apple Sign In and Google Sign In may be used for authentication. These services are governed by their respective privacy policies. We only receive an anonymous identifier — not your name or email.

5. Data Storage and Security

Published constellation data is stored on Supabase servers. We apply reasonable security measures to protect this data. However, please note that published constellations are visible to other users of the app by design — that is the purpose of the shared sky.

Your local game data (progress, settings, unpublished constellations) is stored only on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

6. Children's Privacy

Stargazer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

If you have published constellations and wish to have them removed from the shared sky, please contact us at barinski.m@gmail.com and we will delete them promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
barinski.m@gmail.com
